Research Office — Right Column (UI Contract)

These are the DOM IDs the *production page* must expose so the
right-column logic (resolver → pages referenced strip ↔ memo tokens)
can wire up without guessing.

LEFT / VIEWER (testbed only; prod has the real grid)
- #viewerImg        <img> current page thumbnail (test stub)
- #btnPrev          ◀
- #btnPrimary       primary
- #btnNext          ▶
- #btnAdd           [Add page]
- #status           one-line status text

PAGES REFERENCED STRIP
- #refstrip         container that holds page thumbnails (chips)

PARAGRAPH PREVIEW
- #paraTag          small “#osf-N” label
- #paraPreview      paragraph HTML preview surface

MEMO TO SELF
- #memoTA           <textarea> with the raw memo text
- #chips            inline chips mirroring memo tokens (test-only emphasis)

OPTIONAL (test-only)
- #renderOut        shows the raw memo text; not needed in prod

Token grammar (MUST be preserved):
- live:    [mm|pN=x,y] or [mm|pN=x1,y1:x2,y2]
- deleted: [del]mm|pN=x,y] or [del]mm|pN=x1,y1:x2,y2]
  ^ note: the `[del]` prefix sits *before* `mm|…`
